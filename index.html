Trucks & Pick-Ups. For items larger than a trunk of a compact sedan or weighing more than 200lbs. we offer large trucks of varying sizes. Contact us for details.

Heavy Medical Equipment

Our standard service includes loading & delivering up to 5 items weighing less than 20lbs each.

We may charge an extra fee when:

  • One or more items are more than 50lbs
  • Combined weight of all packages exceed 200lbs
  • Your package is fragile or irregularly shaped
